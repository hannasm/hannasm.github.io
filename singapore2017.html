Singapore

Marina Bay Sands

We stayed in the marina bay sands hotel for 2 nights while acclimating to the 12 hour timezone change. The rooftop pool at the marina bay sands was really cool.

While we were in singapore we visited 4 different hawker centers and spent a good deal of our time wandering around chinatown.

In singapore i did a crossfit drop-in, and ronnie and I both visited an oxygen bar to get our breathe-on.

We spent our last night in a different hotel in Little India and we went swing dancing on the wharf near the marina bay sands.
