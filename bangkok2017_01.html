Bangkok Thailand

Airport

The bangkok airport was pretty impressive between the architecture and the art. We probably experienced the largest culture shock of our trip in thailand. Life was so different from our western / american culture here.

Sala Rattanakosin Bangkok

Along with the KL Journal hotel, this was one the most impressive of the hotels we stayed at while on our trip to asia. The view of the river and Wat Arun was breathtaking and everything was stylish and moderna and cool. The rooftop patio / bar was a nice place to cool off in the evening and the restaraunt downstairs had great breakfast / coffee / lunch options. This was really an enjoyable place to vist.
