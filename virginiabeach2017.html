Virginia Beach

Road Tripping

It was a 13 hour drive from cincinnati, and we didn't have a reservation for the first night of the trip. We ended up getting lucky with a campsight and the scenery was nice and varied.

The Town

We hit up a couple breweries and visited the boardwalk on saturday. It rained pretty hard, off and on, the whole day but we were just planning to hop in the ocean anyway, right? Ruby still needs some help learning to appreciate the water.

The Sun Comes Out

Just in time for sunset, the rain clouds went away and we got to sip champagne on the beach, watching orange and purple hues cast over the horizon. It doesn't count as the honeymoon though!
