Lighting Updates

Lighting was probably one of the most difficult parts of the design because Veronica and Sean had different opinions on what kind of lighting would look best. We also were considering skipping lighting for budget reasons. We ended up installing 7 new lighting circuits including 15 can lights, 15.5 feet of led strips, and 5 ceiling mounted fixtures spanning 3 color temperatures, 2700K, 3000K, and 4200K. Every light is also controlled by dimmer switches so we can set different moods with the lights we have.
