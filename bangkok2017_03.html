Bangkok Thailand

Fitness

While in bangkok i did a crossfit drop-in at Crossfit Bangkok, which is a really cool facility with several floors of fitness space including a outdoor rooftop patio with equipment to do a good variety of workouts. While i was waiting out front of the gym, i saw two pigeons fighting, which i had never seen before. I took this as a motivational blessing from buddha. Ronnie and I also went for a jog in a park near our hotel on the last day.

Khao San Road and Bike Tour

In our first full day in thailand we visited khao san road, where veronica got a henna tattoo. Afterwards we did a four hour foodie bike tour, riding bikes through downtown and eating at various street food stalls.
